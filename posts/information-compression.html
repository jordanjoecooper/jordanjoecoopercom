Information Compression

01/06/2026

[Draft just a playful idea of info compression/decompression applied to sharing of info across a project for any product]

Any medium sized project is fundamentally the movement and compression of information.

Requirements become:

  • Designs
  • Tickets
  • Code
  • Documentation
  • Live Running systems
Here's what often goes wrong:

  • Every handoff loses information.
  • Every abstraction compresses information.
  • Every misunderstanding is decompression failure.


Building any product but especially a software product is not the process of writing code. It is the process of transferring data and understanding between people with minimal loss.
