AI is going to take your job

01, 10, 2026

I still don't believe that this is the case. The better framing is that AI is going to change your job. In the industry I've spent much time in (technology and programming) it has already changed multiple jobs and is continuing to do so at a pace unfamiliar to any humans alive today.

What is changing?

Automation of things that previously required multiple sequential or even parallelhuman inputs to do. Coding is a great example its typically been a job that follows lots of steps and processes, that means large swathes of it can and are being automated with simple AI inputs. However, who is driving the car? You guessed it: The Programmer.

More software is being written than ever before. Much of it by AI but it is still being ultimately created by humans. But, thats a new skill prompting, combining different tooling and patterns is still in your toolbox but if you refuse to adapt as the tools improve or worse you refuse to use them all together then it will cost you your job within five years.
