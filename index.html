Home

I write code and build products. There's something deeply satisfying about taking an idea that doesn't yet exist and through careful thought and deliberate craft, making it real. The best code isn't about what you add. It's about what you remove. Understanding what truly matters and having the discipline to let go of everything else. I've built teams and companies from scratch, and that same principle applies. Start with what's essential. Build with intention. Scale with purpose. This is where I share thoughts on that process and on the craft of turning intention into reality.
