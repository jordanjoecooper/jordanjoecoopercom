Advisory

Currently available

I'm open to a small number of advisory engagements with the right founders at the right stage. Get in touch.

I worked with a small number of founders and early-stage teams, as an advisor, a fractional technical partner, or somewhere in between. The advice came from someone actively in the fire, not looking back at it.

I've been the first engineering hire more than once. I know what it's like to have no playbook, a product that needs to exist before you can sell it, and the pressure of building a team while the product isn't done yet.

When this is useful

You're a founder or co-founder at the early stage: pre-product, early product, or scaling for the first time. The technical decisions feel too big to get wrong and too fast to slow down on. You need someone who's been here, can think alongside you, and will tell you what they actually think.

What we'd work on

  • Strategy: what to build, what to ignore, what order to do things in
  • Technical leadership: CTO-track thinking before or instead of a full-time hire
  • Hiring: who to bring in, when, and how to avoid the early mis-hires that cost months
  • Problem triage: separating what's actually blocking you from what just feels like it is

What I'm not

A generalist consultant who works with anyone. I worked with a small number of people at a time, on problems that were interesting and where the timing was right.
